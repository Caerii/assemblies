# Conclusions

## Defensible Conclusion

In the tested same-brain stim-only regime, multiple stimuli can form distinct
assemblies whose pairwise overlap stays near chance and whose reactivation is
perfect or near-perfect.

## Current Boundaries

- The repo can defend the distinctiveness phenomenon.
- It cannot yet defend a fully settled causal story for every mechanism term.
- The phrase "competition is essential" should therefore be read as a
  qualified empirical takeaway, not as a finished theorem.

## Next Steps

- promote the distinctiveness result into the claims inventory
- design a sharper mechanism-isolation experiment
- compare same-brain and separate-brain controls in one formalized claim
