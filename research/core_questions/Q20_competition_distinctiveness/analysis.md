# Analysis

This question needed a more careful split between phenomenon and mechanism.

Phenomenon:

- yes, the repo has solid evidence that same-brain shared-area training can
  yield distinct assemblies with near-chance overlap and perfect recovery in
  the tested regimes

Mechanism:

- only partly decomposed
- hub-neuron reuse looks small but nonzero
- the quick mechanism experiment did not support every simple story about order
  of presentation or beta

That means the right scientific reading is:

- the distinctiveness phenomenon is real in the documented regime
- the stronger explanatory claim still needs a formal claim document or a
  cleaner targeted mechanism study
