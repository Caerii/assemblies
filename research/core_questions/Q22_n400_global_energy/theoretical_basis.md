# Theoretical Basis

The claim draws on both the repo's internal experiments and the N400 ERP
literature summarized in `research/claims/N400_GLOBAL_ENERGY.md`.

Key references already used in the repo:

- Kutas and Hillyard (1984) on cloze probability and the original N400
- Kutas and Federmeier (2011) on semantic-memory access
- Cheyette and Plaut (2017) on settling dynamics
- Nour Eddine et al. (2024) on prediction-style interpretations

The Assembly Calculus mapping is:

- scalp ERP is an aggregate signal
- the natural AC analogue is aggregate pre-competition energy
- post-k-WTA winners discard precisely the activity mass that aggregate ERP
  measurements care about

This is a stronger theoretical bridge than most other application questions in
the repo because it is already written as a formal claim with evidence,
limitations, and falsification criteria.
