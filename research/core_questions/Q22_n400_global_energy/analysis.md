# Analysis

Q22 is important because it shows what a mature research question looks like in
this repo:

- the question is clearly stated
- the evidence is aggregated across multiple experiments
- limitations are written down explicitly
- there is a falsification section

It also illustrates a broader methodological point: many of the most
interesting readouts are not assembly winners themselves, but measurements taken
at the right internal boundary of the dynamics.

That is one reason the claim is stronger than the older post-k-WTA
interpretations. It matches both the neuroscientific measurement target
(aggregate ERP signal) and the computational boundary in the model
(pre-competition energy).
